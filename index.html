Microcontrollers

Explore popular microcontroller platforms for embedded systems development

Arduino Uno

The Arduino Uno is an open-source microcontroller board based on the ATmega328P. It's perfect for beginners in electronics and coding.

Learn More

ESP32

The ESP32 is a series of low-cost, low-power system on a chip microcontrollers with integrated Wi-Fi and dual-mode Bluetooth.

Learn More

Raspberry Pi

Raspberry Pi is a series of small single-board computers developed to promote teaching of basic computer science in schools.

Learn More
